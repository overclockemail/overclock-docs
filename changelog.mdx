<Update label="November 17, 2025" description="v0.0.4">
**New Overclock AI intelligence layer in beta, UI redesign + more **
  - Overclock AI helps make proactive sales decisions (that users must approve) like suggesting new deals based on email conversations or reminding you to follow up with customers. We've been building this for a while and have released in beta to our users that are interested in trying it!
  - New more modern looking design built to support our agentic features being released soon
  - Allow merging two companies in the companies page
</Update>

  - Adds a new note system for contacts, companies and deals
  - Major UI improvements to the email client and CRM
  - New quick actions menu
</Update>
